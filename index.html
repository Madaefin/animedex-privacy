Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Animedex ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data when you use the Animedex mobile application ("the App"). It also explains your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and related applicable laws.

By creating an account or using the App you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Who We Are

The App is developed and operated by an individual developer based in Romania, European Union. For all data-related inquiries, please contact us at: madalin.simion2000@gmail.com

As our establishment is within the EU, we are subject to the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) directly.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide and improve the App's functionality.

Data type Specific data Required?
Account credentials Email address, hashed password (managed by Firebase) Required to create an account
Profile information Username, profile picture (avatar you upload) Optional — you can use the App without setting these
App activity Anime library entries (watchlist), watch status & progress, preferred genre tags, favourite anime titles Core app functionality — optional per entry
Device & technical identifiers Firebase installation ID, device type/OS version (used for crash reporting and service reliability) Collected automatically

We do not collect location data, contacts, messages, financial information, health data, or any data not listed above.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Account management — to create, authenticate, and secure your account.
  • App functionality — to store and display your anime library, preferences, and profile.
  • Personalisation — to show anime recommendations based on your saved tags and favourites.
  • Service reliability — to diagnose crashes and monitor app performance.

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it for marketing purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses Firebase (provided by Google LLC) for authentication and backend infrastructure. When you sign in, Firebase receives and processes your email address, IP address, and user-agent string as part of the authentication flow.

If you sign in with Google, Google's OAuth service is also involved. Google's privacy practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

No other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are integrated into the App.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account (via the in-app Delete Account option in Settings), all your personal data — including your email, username, profile picture, anime library, and preferences — is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

Firebase may retain authentication logs for a short additional period in accordance with Google's own data retention policies.

7. Data Security

  • All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS / TLS.
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text — Firebase manages password hashing.
  • Access tokens are stored locally on your device and are not shared with third parties beyond Firebase.
  • Profile pictures are stored in a secured server environment with access controls.

In the event of a personal data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours as required by GDPR Art. 33, and will notify affected users without undue delay where the breach is likely to result in high risk (GDPR Art. 34).

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject under GDPR you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — you can correct inaccurate data directly in the App (username, profile picture, preferences) or contact us.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") — you can delete your account and all associated data at any time via Settings → Delete Account.
  • Right to data portability — you can request an export of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to restriction — you can request we restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to object — you can object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent (e.g. profile picture), you can withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
To exercise any of the above rights, contact us at madalin.simion2000@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority. In Romania, this is the Autoritatea Națională de Supraveghere a Prelucrării Datelor cu Caracter Personal (ANSPDCP) .

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at madalin.simion2000@gmail.com and we will delete the data promptly.

Users between 13 and 16 years of age in Romania (and other EU member states that apply the GDPR default age) must have parental or guardian consent to use the App.

Google relies on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission to ensure an adequate level of protection for such transfers. For more information, see Firebase's privacy and security documentation .
